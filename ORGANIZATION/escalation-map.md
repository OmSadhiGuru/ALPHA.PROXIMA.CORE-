# Escalation Map

## Purpose

Fix the one path every Office and Companion follows when a decision exceeds its own authority, and the parallel path that applies specifically to health/wellness/education/complementary-practice content.

## Standard Escalation Path

```
Any Office (per office-map.md, Escalation Path field)
  → LUMIAION (strategic framing, conflict resolution)
    → Founder (final authority, per ../GOVERNANCE/institutional-authority-model.md)
```

Every Office listed in `office-map.md` follows this identical three-step path. No Office escalates directly to the Founder bypassing LUMIAION, except where LUMIAION itself is unavailable or is the source of the conflict being escalated — in which case the Founder is the terminal fallback regardless.

## Ethics & Evidence Parallel Path

For content requiring Ethics & Evidence Council review (Gate 3, Gate 6 — `../INGESTION/ingestion-approval-gates.md`; `../GOVERNANCE/approval-authority-matrix.md`), escalation runs **in parallel**, not in sequence, with the standard path:

```
Office of Health / Office of Nature Systems / any public-facing health-adjacent content
  → LUMIAION (strategic framing)          ┐
  → Ethics & Evidence Council (review)    ┤ both required, in parallel
    → Founder (final approval)            ┘
```

The Founder's approval does not substitute for the Council's review, and the Council's review does not substitute for the Founder's approval — both gates must clear (Standing Gap: the Council is not yet seated, so this path currently stops and waits, per `../GOVERNANCE/approval-authority-matrix.md`).

## Conflict Between Offices

Where two Offices disagree (e.g., Office of Health and Office of Nature Systems on a natural-medicine claim), escalation goes to LUMIAION for conflict resolution (`../GOVERNANCE/institutional-authority-model.md`) before either Office's position reaches the Founder — LUMIAION frames the disagreement as one decision, not two competing escalations.
