# Prompts

## Source Evaluation Prompt

Required inputs:

- Source URL or citation.
- Research question.
- Publication date.
- Author or institution.

Expected output:

- Relevance.
- Reliability.
- Limitations.
- Extracted claims.
- Recommended index entry.
