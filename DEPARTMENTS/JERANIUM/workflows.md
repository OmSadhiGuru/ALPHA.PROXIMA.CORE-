# Workflows

## Offer-to-Distribution Workflow

Input: approved offer, audience, channel, required assets.

Steps:

1. Define target audience.
2. Map offer components.
3. Identify channel requirements.
4. Create distribution checklist.
5. Route public-facing decisions for approval.

Output: distribution plan or approval request.
