# Client Journey & Onboarding
### From first discovery to course completion

*The end-to-end journey map. Each stage names the client's emotional state, the OSG action, the asset used, and the goal. Use it to see where every email, page, and touchpoint fits.*

---

## The full journey (map)

```
DISCOVER → SUBSCRIBE → NURTURE → CONSIDER → ENROLL → ONBOARD → LEARN → INTEGRATE → COMPLETE → ASCEND
```

| Stage | Client state | OSG action | Asset | Goal |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-------|------|
| **1. Discover** | Curious, skeptical | Free guide / content | Lead magnet, socials | Get on the list |
| **2. Subscribe** | Interested | Deliver value fast | Welcome email #1 | Deliver the guide, set tone |
| **3. Nurture** | Warming | Teach, build trust | Welcome sequence | Belief shift: "insight isn't a system" |
| **4. Consider** | Evaluating | Present the offer | Sales page, sales emails | Move to decision |
| **5. Enroll** | Deciding → committed | Frictionless checkout | Checkout, confirmation email | Payment complete |
| **6. Onboard** | Excited + slightly anxious | Reduce overwhelm, set expectations | Onboarding sequence, community invite | Client feels safe & oriented |
| **7. Learn** | Engaged (some drop-off risk) | Guide through modules | Course, live calls, prompts | Weekly progress |
| **8. Integrate** | Doing the work | Support application | Coaching / community / check-ins | Practices stick |
| **9. Complete** | Proud, transformed | Celebrate, capture proof | Completion email, testimonial ask | Graduation + case study |
| **10. Ascend** | Wanting more | Invite next step | Ascension offer | Coaching / next cohort / community |

## Stage detail

### 1–3. Discover → Subscribe → Nurture
- **Lead magnet:** *The 5 Domains of Inner Architecture* (short PDF or email mini-course). Captures email; delivers real value; introduces the framework and the evidence/framework promise.
- **Belief shift to engineer:** "I don't need more insight — I need a system." Every nurture email chips at this.
- **Cadence:** 5-email welcome sequence over ~7 days (see file 05).

### 4. Consider
- Sales page = the Academy page (file 03) expanded, or a dedicated long-form sales page.
- Sales sequence: 4–5 emails over the launch window (see file 05).
- Offer a **discovery call** for coaching-curious prospects.

### 5. Enroll
- **Checkout must be frictionless.** One page, clear price, payment-plan option, visible guarantee.
- Immediately fire the **confirmation email** (see file 05) — payment anxiety is real; confirm fast.

### 6. Onboard (the make-or-break stage)
The first 72 hours after purchase determine completion rates. **[Evidence-based — onboarding & early-win research in behavior design]** The goal is to convert excitement into a first action before doubt creeps in.

**Onboarding checklist delivered to the client:**
1. Confirmation + receipt (instant).
2. "Start here" email within 1 hour: single link, single first action (watch Module 0, 30 min).
3. Community invite with a specific first post prompt.
4. Calendar invites for live calls.
5. Workbook download.
6. Expectation-setting: how much time, how to not fall behind, where to get help.

**Onboarding principle:** *One next action at a time.* Never present the whole course at once — it overwhelms. Reveal Module 1 only after Module 0 is done (or gate by week).

### 7. Learn
- Weekly rhythm: new module Monday, live call midweek, community prompt Friday.
- **Drop-off intervention:** if a client hasn't opened the week's module by day 4, an automated nudge (see follow-up sequence, file 05).
- Celebrate small wins publicly in community.

### 8. Integrate
- The live calls and community are where integration happens. Structure them around *application*, not more content.
- Coaching clients get 1:1 personalization here.

### 9. Complete
- Completion email: celebrate, deliver the "after" Inner Architecture Map, request a testimonial while the transformation is fresh.
- **Testimonial ask is a specific structured prompt**, not "got any feedback?" (template in file 05).

### 10. Ascend
- Course grads are the warmest audience for **Offer 2 (Coaching Container)** and the **next cohort / community paid tier**.
- Time the ascension offer to land at completion, when motivation and proof are highest.

---

## Two anti-drop-off rules (highest ROI in the whole journey)

1. **The first win in 24 hours.** Get every new client to *complete one small thing* within a day of enrolling. Early completion predicts finishing. **[Evidence-based]**
2. **Never more than one next action visible.** Overwhelm is the #1 silent killer of course completion. Sequence, gate, and reveal.
