# OSG Coaching Offers
### Three offers, three transformations, three price points

*Each offer is self-contained and immediately sellable. Prices are recommendations for a newer brand launching in 30 days; raise them as proof accumulates. All offers apply the [Evidence-based] / [Contemplative framework] labeling in delivery.*

---

## Offer 1 — *The Clarity Intensive* (entry / accelerator)

**Format:** 1 session, 90 minutes, 1:1 (+ a 20-min follow-up 2 weeks later)
**Price:** **$250–$450** (recommend launching at **$297**)

### The transformation
> From foggy and stuck on one specific inner knot → to clear, with a concrete next step and a practice to hold it.

A single, deep, structured session that takes one pressing issue — a decision, a recurring pattern, a loss of direction — and moves it. Not open-ended therapy; a focused architecture session.

### What happens
1. Pre-session intake form (the client does the diagnostic thinking before arriving).
2. 90-min session: map the issue, locate the real question, apply one evidence-based tool **[Evidence-based]** and one contemplative inquiry **[Contemplative framework]**, leave with a specific next action + practice.
3. 2-week follow-up to check integration.

### Ideal client
Someone circling one specific problem who doesn't need (or isn't ready for) a long engagement. Great **entry offer** — low commitment, high value, natural gateway to the course or to Offer 2.

### Why it exists in the ladder
Cash flow + trust-building + list-warming. Easiest yes. Converts strangers into clients fast.

---

## Offer 2 — *The Inner Architecture Coaching Container* (core / signature)

**Format:** 3 months, 6 × 60-min 1:1 sessions (bi-weekly) + async support between sessions + full flagship course included
**Price:** **$1,500–$3,000** (recommend launching at **$1,950**)

### The transformation
> From scattered and self-directed-in-theory → to living a structured inner system with personal guidance, in 90 days.

This is the course *plus* a coach walking alongside. The client builds their Inner Architecture with accountability, personalization, and real-time troubleshooting — the difference between reading the map and being guided across the terrain.

### What happens
- Full access to *The Inner Architecture* flagship course (self-paced or cohort).
- 6 bi-weekly 1:1 sessions personalizing each phase to the client's life.
- Async support (voice/text) between sessions for integration.
- A personalized operating rhythm built *with* them, not handed to them.
- Mid-point and end-point review.

### Ideal client
Committed, resourced, at a genuine inflection point. Has tried self-directed growth and knows they do better with structure + a guide. Values depth and is willing to invest in it. This is your **profit center and your best case studies.**

### Why it exists in the ladder
The core margin offer. The course sells it (course grads upgrade); it produces the testimonials that sell everything else.

---

## Offer 3 — *The Deep Work Partnership* (premium / high-touch)

**Format:** 6 months, weekly or bi-weekly 1:1, priority async access, quarterly deep-dive intensives
**Price:** **$4,500–$9,000** (recommend launching at **$6,000**, or **$1,200/mo × 6**)

### The transformation
> From capable-but-fragmented → to a fundamentally reorganized inner life, with a coach as sustained thinking partner over half a year.

For people doing a *major* life restructuring — a founder rebuilding after burnout, someone reinventing after a rupture, a high-performer who has hit the ceiling of willpower and needs a different foundation. Deep, sustained, personal.

### What happens
- Everything in Offer 2, extended and deepened over 6 months.
- Weekly rhythm for momentum; quarterly longer intensives for reorientation.
- Priority async access — the coach is genuinely *in* the client's process.
- A fully bespoke inner operating system, iterated over 6 months of real life.

### Ideal client
High-agency, well-resourced, facing a large and real transition. Wants a trusted partner for the whole arc, not a course. **Low volume, high value** — a handful of these anchor the business.

### Why it exists in the ladder
Anchors the price ceiling (makes Offer 2 feel reasonable), and serves the clients who want the most. Even 3–4 partnerships a year is meaningful revenue.

## Pricing guidance for the anxious founder

- **Underpricing is the more common error**, not overpricing — but launch prices should be *slightly* uncomfortable-low so the first cohort/clients say yes and generate proof.
- Raise Offer 2 by ~25% after the first 3 paying clients and a testimonial each.
- Payment plans dramatically raise conversion. Offer them on Offers 2 and 3 (e.g., 3× or 6× monthly).
- Never discount the transformation; if needed, adjust *scope* (fewer sessions) not *value*.
