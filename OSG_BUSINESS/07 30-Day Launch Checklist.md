# 30-Day Launch Checklist
### Ship the OSG Business Foundation in 30 days

*Optimized for shipping, not perfection. The guiding rule: **launch a live cohort on the strength of Modules 0–2 + the transformation promise. Build the rest while teaching it.** You do not need a finished product to open the doors.*

---

## The one-line strategy

> Warm the list in Weeks 1–2, soft-launch to warm leads in Week 3, public-launch and enroll in Week 4 — teaching the first cohort live starting the following week.

---

## WEEK 1 — Foundation & Offer (decide and lock)

**Goal: the offer exists and can be paid for.**

- [ ] Lock flagship course name, promise, price, cohort start date
- [ ] Lock the 3 coaching offers + launch prices (file 02)
- [ ] Write/publish the Academy sales page (use file 03 Academy + FAQ)
- [ ] Set up checkout (Stripe or course platform) — with a payment-plan option
- [ ] Set up email tool; create the list + lead-magnet automation
- [ ] Create the lead magnet — *The 5 Domains of Inner Architecture* (short PDF; can be 3–5 pages)
- [ ] Publish Home + About pages (file 03)
- [ ] Set up a booking link for discovery calls / Clarity Intensives

**Week 1 ships:** a live sales page + working checkout + a way to collect emails. *That alone means you can sell.*

> ⚠️ Note: Stripe (and some tools) may need you to authorize/connect the account before automation works — do that early in Week 1 so it's not a Week-4 blocker.

---

## WEEK 2 — Content & Nurture (produce the minimum, warm the list)

**Goal: enough course to teach Week 1, and a list being warmed.**

- [ ] Record Module 0 (Orientation) + Module 1 (Attention) — video + practice
- [ ] Draft Module 2; outline Modules 3–8 (outlines are enough — you'll build them live)
- [ ] Build the Inner Architecture Workbook — Module 0–2 sections (rest can follow weekly)
- [ ] Load the Welcome Sequence (file 05A) into your email tool
- [ ] Load the Confirmation + Onboarding emails (file 05B/C)
- [ ] Open the **waitlist**; start driving traffic to the lead magnet
- [ ] Post consistently on your channel(s) — teach the "insight isn't a system" belief
- [ ] Set up the community (file 06) — Start Here + Introductions + seed posts

**Week 2 ships:** Modules 0–1 exist, emails are automated, the waitlist is collecting.

---

## WEEK 3 — Soft Launch (sell to the warm)

**Goal: first paying students from your warmest audience.**

- [ ] Finalize Module 2; stage Modules 3–8 for live weekly delivery
- [ ] Load the Sales Sequence (file 05E) + Follow-up Sequence (file 05D)
- [ ] **Soft-launch to the waitlist + warm list** — early-bird price or bonus for first cohort
- [ ] Personally invite anyone who's had a discovery call / Clarity Intensive
- [ ] Enroll first students; run confirmation → onboarding → community flow live
- [ ] Reply personally to every community intro (file 06, Step 4)
- [ ] Fix whatever breaks (there will be something — that's fine)

**Week 3 ships:** real enrollments, real onboarding tested on real humans.

---

## WEEK 4 — Public Launch (open to everyone)

**Goal: full public launch + cohort filled.**

- [ ] Public open-cart announcement (email + socials)
- [ ] Run the full 5-email Sales Sequence over the cart-open window
- [ ] Host a free live intro session / masterclass to drive enrollment (optional but high-ROI)
- [ ] Offer discovery calls for coaching-curious leads (upsell to Offer 2)
- [ ] Close cart with a real deadline (Email 5 = last call)
- [ ] Onboard the full cohort; confirm live-call schedule
- [ ] **Cohort teaching begins the following Monday** — build Modules 3–8 live, week by week

**Week 4 ships:** a launched business with paying clients and a live cohort running.

---

## The "minimum to launch" bar (print this)

You are ready to open the doors when you have:

1. ✅ A sales page
2. ✅ A working checkout (with payment plan)
3. ✅ A way to collect emails + a lead magnet
4. ✅ Module 0 + Module 1 produced
5. ✅ Confirmation + onboarding emails automated
6. ✅ A community space with a Start-Here + intro flow
7. ✅ A cohort start date

**Everything else can be built in public, while teaching.** Do not wait for Modules 3–8. Do not wait for a perfect website. Do not wait to feel ready.

---

## Post-launch (Week 5+, for reference)

- [ ] Deliver the cohort live; record each module as you teach
- [ ] Run retention/drop-off nudges (file 05D)
- [ ] Collect testimonials at completion (file 05F)
- [ ] Raise Offer 2 price after first 3 clients + testimonials
- [ ] Turn the recorded cohort into the evergreen self-paced product for launch #2
- [ ] Invite grads to ascend → coaching / next cohort / paid community tier

---

## Founder mindset notes (tape to your wall)

- **Done and shipped beats perfect and unshipped.** Every day.
- **The first cohort is a co-creation, not a finished product.** Price it accordingly, tell them so, over-deliver on care.
- **Your list is the business.** Everything in Weeks 1–2 that grows and warms the list is the real work.
- **One next action.** For you, and for every client. Always just the next one.
