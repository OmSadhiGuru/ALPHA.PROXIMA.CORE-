# Title

> [!research-question] Research Question
> What is being asked.

## Hypothesis

Tentative answer — explicitly unconfirmed.

## Context

Why this question matters now; what prior work it builds on.

## Evidence Type

Primary Intellectual Honesty category this study produces.

## Method

How the observation or data was obtained.

## Observation

What was directly seen, measured, or reported — personal observation register only, see the Five-Register Separation.

## Data

Structured results, where applicable.

## Interpretation

What the observer believes the data means — kept explicitly separate from Observation.

## Limitations

What this study cannot establish.

## Risks

Any risk the study or its subject matter introduces, including risk of misuse if published.

> [!ethics-review-required]
> State explicitly if this study requires Ethics & Evidence Council review before any public step. If not applicable, state why.

## Founder Reflection

> [!reflection]
> The Founder's own synthesis — kept distinct from Interpretation.

## Next Experiment

Proposed follow-up, if any.

## Related Knowledge Objects

- Linked list.

## Publication Potential

> [!public-content-candidate]
> Whether this study is a candidate for OSGMETAPHYSICS publication, and what would need to happen first.
