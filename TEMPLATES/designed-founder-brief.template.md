# Founder Brief — Date

## Pending Decisions

- Decision awaiting Founder input, with link to source document.

## Institutional Health

Roll-up signal — vault integrity, repository consistency, drift indicators.

## Repository Updates

- What changed since the last brief, by repository.

## Knowledge Updates

- New or revised knowledge objects since the last brief.

## Business Updates

- Office of Enterprise status: CRM, leads, funnels, offers, sales, marketing, dashboards, client journey.

## Research Updates

- Office of Research / Office of Knowledge status.

## Risks

> [!warning]
