## Scientific Perspective

Explain the claim through scientific, psychological, behavioral, physiological, systems, or evidence-based framing where applicable.

If no scientific framing is available, state the limitation.

## Metaphysical Perspective

Explain the claim through the relevant metaphysical, symbolic, energetic, archetypal, or consciousness-based framing.

Separate interpretation from source evidence.
