# Handoff Log

Question answered: What structured production handoffs have occurred?

No conversational updates. Only structured handoffs.

## Universal Handoff Protocol

Every department must define:

- What enters: accepted input from previous queue or department.
- What leaves: completed output or decision-ready package.
- Quality criteria: conditions that must be true before handoff.
- Verification: evidence, test, review, source, or checklist used.
- Next destination: queue or department receiving the output.

| Department | What Enters | What Leaves | Quality Criteria | Verification | Next Destination |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Executive Office | Founder direction, approval requests, roadmap options | Prioritized decisions and approved constraints | Clear decision, owner, and next queue | Approval gate or dashboard update | Backlog or active queue |
| Research Office | Research questions, unknowns, source needs | Evidence summary and source list | Sources named, uncertainty stated, relevance clear | Source review | Blueprint Queue |
