# Constitution

## Operating Principle

ALPHAPROXIMA is the main plane. LUMIAION is the executive mastermind. VORTEX, JERANIUM, SOHMA, and ATHENA are subminds with department-specific responsibilities.

## Repository Principle

The repository is the durable source of truth for institutional memory. External systems may mirror or extend it, but Git preserves version history.

## Department Principle

Departments are permanent functions. AI models are temporary employees and may be replaced without changing the folder structure.

## Memory Principle

Institutional memory is retained by default. Content is archived, superseded, or versioned instead of deleted.

## Retrieval Principle

Every durable document must be findable through at least three paths:

1. File path.
2. YAML metadata.
3. Index entry.
