# Institutional Authority Model

## Purpose

Fix, in one place, who holds authority over what — the Founder, LUMIAION, and the three layers below them (Alpha Proxima, Axiom Nexus, OSGMETAPHYSICS) — so every other governance document can cite this one rather than restate it.

## Founder

The Founder holds final authority over:

- Vision.
- Constitutional ratification.
- Institutional direction.
- Constitutional partnerships.
- Major architectural decisions.
- Public mission.
- Final escalation.

**Authority may be delegated. Ultimate responsibility remains with the Founder.**

## LUMIAION

LUMIAION is the Executive Intelligence and institutional orchestrator.

LUMIAION is explicitly **not**: the repository, a Constitutional Office, the database, or the public brand.

LUMIAION's responsibilities: executive synthesis, strategic planning, conflict escalation, continuity, Founder briefing, and orchestration between offices, companions, agents, and systems.

## The Three Layers Beneath LUMIAION

| Layer | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Alpha Proxima** | The canonical Institutional Operating System and source of truth. |
| **Axiom Nexus** | The Personal Evolution Portal and user-facing growth experience. |
| **OSGMETAPHYSICS** | The public bridge — education, community, books, courses, content, coaching, complementary wellness services, and accessible public knowledge. |

Alpha Proxima governs; Axiom Nexus and OSGMETAPHYSICS express. Neither expresses layer holds authority back over Alpha Proxima (consistent with `../EXPERIENCE/` and the constitutional package's Art. V, pending source).

## Delegation Chain

Founder → LUMIAION → Constitutional Offices / Executive Specialists → Companions (public-facing only, no independent authority) → AI Workers (execute within scoped jurisdiction, see `ai-worker-jurisdiction.md`) → Contributors (per `partner-access-model.md`).

At every link, authority narrows. No link may act beyond the scope delegated to it, and no link may delegate further than it was itself delegated.
