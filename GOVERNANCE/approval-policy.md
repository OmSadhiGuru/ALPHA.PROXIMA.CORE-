# Approval Policy

## Requires Founder Approval

- Strategic positioning.
- Public brand direction.
- Business model decisions.
- External publication.
- Legal, privacy, or data-risk decisions.
- Irreversible deletions or migrations.
- Paid infrastructure or vendor commitments.

## Does Not Require Founder Approval

- Documentation formatting.
- Metadata corrections.
- Index updates.
- Research summaries.
- Draft options.
- Internal prompts.
- Non-destructive file organization.

## Approval Records

Approval decisions should be logged in `LOGS/` or the operating office approval register when active.
