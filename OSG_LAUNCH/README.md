# OSG Launch Technical Foundation

## Purpose

This workspace contains the production-ready technical foundation for the OSG launch.

It is intentionally isolated from Alpha Proxima institutional infrastructure. OSG public business systems, clients, content, automation, and operations should remain separate from Alpha Proxima constitutional, research, and governance materials.

## Operating Boundary

- OSG is public business infrastructure.
- Alpha Proxima remains private institutional infrastructure.
- Do not mix clients, revenue systems, public content workflows, or customer data into Alpha Proxima folders.
- Do not publish Alpha Proxima constitutional material through OSG systems unless explicitly approved through the appropriate institutional process.

## Implementation Notes

This foundation is designed for launch speed and later scale. It avoids heavy architecture until there is actual operational pressure.
