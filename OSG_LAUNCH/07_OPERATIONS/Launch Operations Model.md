# OSG Launch Operations Model

## Purpose

Define how OSG should operate during launch.

## Daily Loop

- Review bookings, payments, leads, and client tasks.
- Move content through the pipeline.
- Resolve blockers.
- Record decisions that affect launch execution.

## Weekly Loop

- Review metrics.
- Review content performance.
- Review course production.
- Review client pipeline.
- Review automation failures.
- Choose next week priorities.

## Launch Metrics

Track only the metrics needed for action:

- leads captured
- calls booked
- calls completed
- purchases
- active clients
- content published
- email subscribers
- revenue
- unresolved blockers

## Decision Log

Record decisions that change:

- offer
- pricing
- audience
- launch date
- platform
- automation
- client delivery
