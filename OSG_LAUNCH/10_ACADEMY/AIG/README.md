# Awaken the Inner Guru Production Folder

## Purpose

This is the active production folder for `AIG - Awaken the Inner Guru`.

## Course Meaning

The course teaches learners to stop outsourcing their most important questions and build a grounded relationship with their own inner guidance.

The course does not give answers. It trains the learner to reflect, observe, discern, practice, integrate, and act with inner authority.
