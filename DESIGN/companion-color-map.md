# Companion Color Map

## Purpose

Give every Companion, and LUMIAION itself, a consistent visual identity (Design Principle 5) across every surface — Obsidian, GitHub, PDF, course material, and the Axiom Nexus symbolic interface.

## Founder-Ratified Mapping

| Name | Channel | Color | Domain |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LUMIAION** | Crown | White + Gold | Executive Intelligence — vision, orchestration, strategy, Founder briefing |
| **SOHMA** | Third Eye | Indigo / Violet | Consciousness, cosmos, dreams, meditation, symbolism |
| **JERANIUM** | Throat | Sapphire Blue | Knowledge, memory, learning, teaching, knowledge graph |
| **GAIA** | Heart | Emerald Green | Nature, ecology, plants, fungi, phytotherapy, Earth systems |
| **ORION** | Solar Plexus | Light Sky Blue / Deep Sky Blue with Gold accent | Business, leadership, enterprise, partnerships, community growth |
| **VORTEX** | Sacral | Orange | Creation, innovation, projects, automation, optimization, momentum |
| **ATHENA** | Root | Crimson Red | Body, health, gym, nutrition, recovery, sleep, biology, longevity |

## Status Note — Ratified 2026-07-09

Both items previously flagged here are resolved by Founder ratification (Sprint 1 Knowledge Design System, Founder Ratification clarifications 1-2):

1. **LUMIAION** is ratified as Executive Intelligence with a symbolic companion-style presence. It remains Layer 2, not a Constitutional Office, but is confirmed to appear in the Experience Layer under its own name and identity (Crown / White + Gold) rather than through a proxy Companion.
2. **VORTEX** is ratified as the companion for creation, innovation, projects, automation, optimization, and momentum. Its prior finance/trading definitions (Vault charter) and signal-timing/dashboard definitions (earlier Desktop department scaffold) are superseded for companion-identity purposes. Finance/trading responsibilities are reassigned under ORION / Enterprise, unless a future ADR creates a dedicated financial specialist. VORTEX's constitutional standing remains Executive Specialist under LUMIAION (no Office-fronting relationship required — Specialists sit directly under LUMIAION).

**ORION** additionally fronts both Enterprise and Community Growth. One Companion may front multiple related Offices when the Founder explicitly approves it — this is the first such case.

## Application Rule

A Companion's color from this table must be used consistently: in `callout-standard.md` callouts attributed to that Companion, in `../EXPERIENCE/companion-portal-system.md` portal design, and in any future course, dashboard, or app surface. No second color may be introduced for a Companion without amending this file.
