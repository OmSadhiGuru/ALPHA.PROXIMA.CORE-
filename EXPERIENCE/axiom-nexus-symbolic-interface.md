# Axiom Nexus — Symbolic Interface

## Purpose

Define Axiom Nexus's primary symbolic interface: what it depicts, how it is navigated, and — critically — what it is not.

## Definition

Axiom Nexus is the Personal Evolution Portal (Constitution Art. IX, §1). Its Symbolic Mode is one of three required interface modes (`experience-modes.md`) and the only one built on metaphor rather than direct data or professional presentation.

## Interface Elements

1. **Living human silhouette** — the central navigation object. Represents the individual's evolving engagement with the system over time, not a literal body.
2. **Chakra-channel companion portals** — the seven identities in `../DESIGN/companion-color-map.md` (LUMIAION, SOHMA, JERANIUM, GAIA, ORION, VORTEX, ATHENA) are positioned along the silhouette at their assigned channel (Crown, Third Eye, Throat, Heart, Solar Plexus, Sacral, Root respectively), each rendered in its assigned color.
3. **Flowing channels** — visual connections between portals, representing the relationships between domains, not a claim about physical energy pathways.
4. **Progression visualization** — the silhouette's evolution reflects engagement and growth within the system over time.
5. **Toroidal field expansion** — an ambient visualization representing the whole system's activity or the individual's expanding engagement; decorative-functional, not diagnostic.
6. **Companion access through portals** — a user reaches a Companion, and through it an Office or Specialist, by engaging its portal on the silhouette. This is the mechanism defined in `companion-portal-system.md`.

## Constitutional Limit — Read Before Implementing

**This is symbolic navigation and personal growth visualization. It must not be presented as medical anatomy or diagnostic truth.**

This limit is binding, not stylistic:
- No element of this interface may be labeled, captioned, or implied to represent actual anatomy, physiology, or a medical/diagnostic assessment of the user.
- Any content reached through this interface that touches health (Office of Health, Office of Nature Systems) must carry the Medical Safety Note callout (`../DESIGN/callout-standard.md`) at the point of access, not only within the underlying document.
- Chakra terminology here is used as an organizing metaphor for the Companion system's visual layout, consistent with the Office of Consciousness's constitutional distinction between symbolic/traditional and scientific framing (Constitution Art. V implementing text) — it does not assert the chakra system as scientific or medical fact, and this interface must not imply otherwise.
