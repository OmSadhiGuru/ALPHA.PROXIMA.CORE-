# Alpha Proxima — Neural Interface

## Purpose

Define Alpha Proxima's own interface metaphor, distinct from and never mixed with Axiom Nexus's body/silhouette interface (`axiom-nexus-symbolic-interface.md`).

## Definition

Where Axiom Nexus visualizes an individual's personal evolution, Alpha Proxima (Layer 3, Constitution Art. IV) visualizes the institution's own knowledge and memory. This is the interface a Constitutional Partner, Core Contributor, or the Founder uses to work *with* the institution, not to experience OSGMETAPHYSICS as a member of the public.

## Interface Elements

1. **Living brain** — the overall metaphor for Alpha Proxima as an active, growing intelligence, distinct from a static archive.
2. **Knowledge graph** — the literal structure: nodes and typed relationships, consistent with any existing knowledge-graph tooling this institution has already built (node/relationship taxonomy, node/relationship registries) and not a competing reinvention of it.
3. **Nodes** — individual knowledge objects, Offices, Companions, research programs, and decisions, each a distinct point in the graph.
4. **Pathways** — the relationships between nodes: supports, contradicts, derives-from, produced-by, and other typed relationships consistent with existing relationship taxonomy work.
5. **Research connections** — pathways specific to the Office of Research and Office of Knowledge's function, showing how a claim traces back to its raw source (Constitution Art. X, Knowledge Principle).
6. **Companion collaboration** — how Companions' fronted Offices interact and hand off work to one another, visualized as graph activity rather than narrative.
7. **Institutional memory** — the persistent, versioned record beneath the graph — what makes the graph durable rather than a live-only visualization.

## Constitutional Limit — Read Before Implementing

**Do not mix Alpha Proxima with the Axiom Nexus body interface.** No silhouette, chakra channel, or body metaphor may appear in the Alpha Proxima neural interface, and no knowledge-graph/node/pathway visualization may appear inside Axiom Nexus's symbolic mode. The two interfaces serve two different layers (Constitution Art. IV vs. Art. IX) and must remain visually and conceptually separable at all times, including in any future UI where both are reachable from the same account.
