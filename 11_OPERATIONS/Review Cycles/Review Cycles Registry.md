# Review Cycles Registry

## Purpose

Define recurring operational rhythms that keep the Foundation coherent across days, weeks, months, quarters, and years.

---

## Scope

This registry defines operational review inputs and outputs. It does not force decisions; it creates reliable moments for humans and offices to decide.

## Core Content

| Cycle | Purpose | Required Participants | Inputs | Outputs | Metrics Reviewed | Expected Decisions |
|-------|---------|-----------------------|--------|---------|------------------|-------------------|
| Daily | Preserve continuity and catch operational drift | Founder if active, LUMIAION, Engineering or Observatory as needed | Current priorities, validation reports, open queues, recent notes | Daily priority note, routed tasks, blockers | Broken links, open tasks, active workflows, automation failures | What needs attention today? |
| Weekly | Consolidate work and reduce drift | LUMIAION, Engineering, Observatory, relevant offices | Week changes, reports, proposals, research updates | Weekly operations summary, prioritized queue | Vault health, research debt, engineering debt, proposal movement | What should move next week? |
| Monthly | Review office health and backlog maturity | LUMIAION, office leads, Founder as needed | Office reports, metrics, future queue, active projects | Monthly operational review | Office throughput, stale artifacts, link health, metadata debt | What should be escalated, archived, or resourced? |
| Quarterly | Strategic alignment and architecture review | Founder, LUMIAION, Architecture, Engineering, Research, Observatory | Quarterly metrics, architecture gaps, research progress, future proposals | Quarterly review packet, ADR candidates, roadmap updates | Institutional health, research progress, engineering platform maturity | What changes require architecture or governance review? |
| Annual | Institutional memory and long-horizon continuity | Founder, LUMIAION, all offices | Year archive, decisions, research, operational metrics, future office | Annual state of Foundation report | Growth, continuity, debt, resilience, strategic alignment | What must change for the next year? |
