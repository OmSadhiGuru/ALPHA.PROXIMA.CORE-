# Institutional Translation Template v1.0

*This template is completed once for every Canonical Research Program, following Stage 9 (Canonical Integration). It is not a summary of the research. It is the institutional record of how the research changed the Foundation. Complete all sections. For deferred dimensions, document the deferral explicitly. A Translation without explicit deferrals is incomplete.*
