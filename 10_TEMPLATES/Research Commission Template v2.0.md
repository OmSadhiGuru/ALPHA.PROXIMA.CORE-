## Acceptance Criteria

*What must be true for this commission to be declared complete? These criteria are the test. If they cannot be checked, they are not criteria.*

### Quality Criteria

- [ ] Institutional principle is clearly stated and defensible from the evidence
- [ ] Every empirical claim carries DOC-ID attribution and evidence class
- [ ] All genuine disagreements in the literature are preserved (no false consensus)
- [ ] All open questions are explicitly acknowledged
- [ ] Canonical Synthesis reviewed and acknowledged by Founder
- [ ] Research Debt Register updated with all identified gaps

### Completeness Criteria

- [ ] All required deliverables produced and committed to Vault
- [ ] All program-specific deliverables produced and committed
- [ ] Minimum 20 frameworks in Theory Matrix
- [ ] Minimum 30 terms in Canonical Glossary
- [ ] Minimum 8 Concept Notes
- [ ] Cross-program connections identified and documented

### Constitutional Criteria

- [ ] No canonical claim contradicts existing Foundation constitutional documents (or contradiction documented and escalated)
- [ ] Evidence classification system applied consistently
- [ ] ARCHIVE documents immutably committed
- [ ] Ethics Council review completed (if applicable)

## About This Template

This is Research Commission Template v2.0, ratified under FD-004 (Epoch IV — Institutional Research Framework). It supersedes any prior informal commission formats.

Key changes from informal practice (v1 implicit):
- Formal Research Function assignment table (six functions; independence protocol explicit)
- Expanded acceptance criteria (quality + completeness + constitutional)
- Canonical Destination section (explicit connection to existing knowledge graph)
- Program-specific deliverables section (customizable per commission)
- Scope discipline section (in scope / out of scope / domain boundaries)
