# RP-001 — Objectives

---

## Program Objectives

### Objective 1 — Map the Theoretical Landscape
Produce a canonical comparative framework of all major theories of consciousness, documenting their core claims, mechanisms, empirical evidence, weaknesses, and mutual relationships.

**Success criterion:** Theory Matrix covering at minimum 7 theories across 9 analytical dimensions. Status: ✓ Complete — [[10 Theory Matrix/RP-001 Theory Matrix]]

### Objective 2 — Establish Institutional Position
Develop Alpha Proxima's canonical synthesis: what is agreed, what is contested, what is open, and what the Foundation's research posture will be.

**Success criterion:** Canonical Synthesis with full evidence classification, reviewed and ratified by the Research Council. Status: ✓ Draft complete — [[09 Canonical Synthesis/RP-001 Canonical Synthesis]] (under_review)

### Objective 3 — Build the Research Knowledge Graph
Create a network of interconnected concept notes that allow non-specialist researchers to navigate consciousness science systematically.

**Success criterion:** 11 concept notes, bidirectionally linked, each with evidence classification and institutional notes. Status: In progress — [[13 Research Graph/RP-001 Research Graph]]

### Objective 4 — Identify Translational Opportunities
Determine which theoretical frameworks are most actionable for LUMIAION and Alpha Proxima's AI research direction.

**Success criterion:** Documented translational assessment for each theory in the Theory Matrix. Status: Partial — embedded in [[08 Comparative Framework/RP-001 Comparative Framework]]

### Objective 5 — Establish Research Infrastructure
Build the institutional infrastructure (source registry, evidence registry, archive, open questions register) that will support ongoing research.

**Success criterion:** All infrastructure documents created and linked. Status: In progress

## Phase Structure

### Phase 1 (Current) — Foundation and Synthesis
**Scope:** External literature review; theory mapping; canonical synthesis; knowledge graph  
**Duration:** 2026-Q3  
**Outputs:** Canonical Synthesis, Theory Matrix, Comparative Framework, Knowledge Graph, Evidence Registry, Source Registry

### Phase 2 — Adversarial Collaboration Monitoring
**Scope:** Track COGITATE and INTREPID results; update canonical synthesis as new results emerge  
**Trigger:** Publication of new adversarial collaboration results  
**Outputs:** Synthesis revisions; updated Evidence Registry

### Phase 3 — Contemplative Integration (Candidate)
**Scope:** Integrate insights from Advaita Vedanta, Buddhist phenomenology, and Yogic frameworks  
**Status:** Under evaluation — see Open Questions
