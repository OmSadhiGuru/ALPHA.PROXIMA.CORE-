# Global Neuronal Workspace Theory (GNWT)

---

## Core Claim

Consciousness arises when information is *globally broadcast* across a distributed neural network — the Global Neuronal Workspace — making it available to multiple cognitive processes simultaneously. Unconscious processing is local; conscious processing is the ignition of a global network. [Evidence class: M — DOC-003, p.4]

---

## Mechanism

1. **Local processing:** Sensory information is processed in specialized cortical modules (visual, auditory, somatosensory)
2. **Ignition threshold:** When a stimulus is sufficiently salient or attended, local processing reaches an ignition threshold
3. **Global broadcast:** A sudden, non-linear ignition event broadcasts information across the fronto-parietal workspace
4. **Global availability:** The broadcasted information becomes available to working memory, report systems, attention, and action planning

---

## Anatomical Basis

- **Primary locus:** Fronto-parietal networks — prefrontal cortex + parietal cortex
- **Long-range connections:** Pyramidal neurons with long-range axons connect distant cortical regions
- **Working memory gates:** Prefrontal areas maintain information in conscious access

---

## Relationship to Triple Network Model

In the Triple Network Model, GNWT primarily maps to the **Executive Control Network (ECN)** — the fronto-parietal ignition constitutes conscious synthesis. [DOC-004, p.2]

---

## Empirical Support

- Neuroimaging shows sudden, widespread fronto-parietal activation at perceptual threshold
- EEG shows late (>300ms) global "ignition" responses accompanying conscious reports
- Supports predictions in no-report paradigms partially (COGITATE results mixed) [Emerging evidence — DOC-003, p.2, p.15]

---

## Weakness

- COGITATE adversarial testing challenged the necessity of prefrontal activity for conscious experience (vs. report)
- Does not explain *why* global broadcast gives rise to subjective experience — the hard problem remains
- May explain *access* consciousness (reportability) without explaining *phenomenal* consciousness

---

## Alpha Proxima Institutional Position

GNWT and IIT are treated as **complementary architectural layers**, not competing theories. GNWT governs **functional routing capacity** — what information becomes consciously accessed at a given moment. [Class E — DOC-004, p.3]

---

## AI Implications

GNWT is among the most computationally tractable theories for AI. A global workspace architecture — a shared, bottleneck information space accessible to multiple specialized modules — is naturally expressible in computational terms. [DOC-003, p.16]
