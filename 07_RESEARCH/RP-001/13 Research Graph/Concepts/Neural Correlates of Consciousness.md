# Neural Correlates of Consciousness (NCCs)

---

## Core Definition

A **Neural Correlate of Consciousness** is the minimal set of neural mechanisms jointly sufficient for a specific conscious percept. Given a specific conscious experience (e.g., seeing a face), an NCC is the smallest set of neural events that are both necessary and sufficient for that experience to occur. [Evidence class: C — DOC-003, p.3]

---

## Why NCCs Matter

The NCC research program is the empirical backbone of consciousness science. It does not presuppose a theory — it identifies the neural signatures of consciousness empirically, leaving the theoretical interpretation open. NCCs are the primary data that theories of consciousness must explain.

---

## Types of NCCs

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Content-specific NCCs** | Neural patterns that vary with the specific content of consciousness (e.g., orientation-specific activity in V1 for visual orientation) |
| **Background NCCs** | Neural states that enable consciousness in general, without determining its content (e.g., arousal systems, thalamo-cortical loops) |

---

## Key Research Paradigms

| Paradigm | Method | What It Isolates |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| Binocular rivalry | Two different images, one per eye; perception alternates | NCCs for conscious percept vs. suppressed image |
| Masking | Brief stimulus followed by mask; vary visibility | NCCs for visible vs. invisible stimuli |
| No-report paradigms | Measure NCCs without requiring behavioral report | NCCs for experience independent of report |
| Anesthesia studies | Compare conscious vs. unconscious states | NCCs for global conscious access |

---

## What Is Established

The existence of NCCs — that specific, identifiable brain states reliably accompany specific conscious experiences — is **among the most replicated findings in consciousness science**. [Class C — DOC-003, p.3]

This is one of the four consensus claims in the RP-001 Canonical Synthesis.

---

## What Is Contested

- Which specific structures are necessary vs. sufficient (prefrontal debate — GNWT vs. RPT/IIT)
- Whether NCCs are the *cause* of consciousness or merely its *correlate*
- How to identify NCCs without presupposing a theory of what consciousness is
