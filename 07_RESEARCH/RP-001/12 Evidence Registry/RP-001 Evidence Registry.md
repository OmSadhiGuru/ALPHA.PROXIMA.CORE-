# RP-001 — Evidence Registry

---

## Purpose

This registry is the authoritative record of every claim in RP-001's institutional knowledge base, its evidence classification, and its source trace. It is the operationalization of [[Book III - Knowledge Integrity]], Art. II.

Every claim in the [[09 Canonical Synthesis/RP-001 Canonical Synthesis]] must appear here.

---

## Evidence Classification System

Per [[Book III - Knowledge Integrity]], Art. II:

| Class | Label | Definition |
|-------|-------|------------|
| **C** | Consensus | Established evidence; repeated, broadly accepted findings |
| **M** | Competing Models | Genuine empirical and conceptual competition between frameworks |
| **E** | Emerging Evidence | Significant but not yet replicated at scale |
| **Q** | Open Question | Tractable but unresolved; no current empirical resolution |
| **S** | Speculative | Coherent hypothesis; minimal or no empirical support |
| **P** | Phenomenological | First-person report; not subject to third-person empirical validation in the standard sense |
