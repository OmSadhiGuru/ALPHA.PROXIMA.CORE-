## What This Program Is

RP-001 is Alpha Proxima's first formal research program. Its purpose is to build a structured, epistemically rigorous knowledge base on human consciousness — integrating scientific, philosophical, and phenomenological perspectives — that serves as the epistemic foundation for SOHMA and as a model for all future research programs.

The Atlas does not assert a single theory of consciousness as correct. It maps the territory.

## Current State of Knowledge — Summary

The following is the institutional position on the state of consciousness science as of the first research phase. Full treatment is in [[RP-001 Canonical Synthesis]].

**What is agreed [Established evidence]:**
- No single theory of consciousness is complete. Each captures a fragment of the architecture.
- Recurrent neural dynamics and global information broadcast are both associated with conscious access.
- Predictive processing principles are pervasive across cognition; their specific role in consciousness is not yet settled.

**What is contested [Competing Models — Class M]:**
- Whether consciousness requires global broadcast (GNWT) or can arise locally in recurrent sensory cortex (RPT)
- Whether integrated information (IIT's Φ) captures the structure of experience
- Whether prefrontal cortex is necessary for consciousness or only for report and metacognition

**What remains open [Class Q]:**
- The hard problem: why physical processes are accompanied by subjective experience at all
- Minimal conditions for machine consciousness
- Whether contemplative traditions' phenomenological maps correspond to the states identified by neuroscience

**Alpha Proxima's institutional posture:**
An integrative, meta-framework stance. The seven major theories (GNWT, IIT, PP, RPT, HOT, AST, Active Inference) are treated as complementary lenses — not competing dogmas. No theory is declared the winner. Uncertainty is preserved, not resolved. [Theoretical interpretation per SanaLab DOC-003]

## The Critical Architectural Insight

From SanaLab DOC-004 (GNWT vs. IIT Deep Dive):

> *"GNWT accurately models the access and maintenance of conscious content during high-level cognitive operations (the processing timeline). Conversely, IIT accurately models the presence and intensity of experience itself within structural neural fields (the background state). Our systems model must treat them not as mutually exclusive truths, but as complementary architectural layers: IIT governing the structural substrate capacity, and GNWT governing functional routing capacity."* [Emerging evidence per DOC-004]

This is the foundational insight that organises how Alpha Proxima will build its consciousness research architecture.
