# RP-001 — Research Graph

---

## Purpose

This document maps the conceptual structure of RP-001 — how the major domains, frameworks, and questions relate to each other. It is a navigational and analytical tool: it shows where the program has coverage, where gaps exist, and how any new research note should connect to existing knowledge.

The Research Graph is maintained by [[JERANIUM Charter|JERANIUM]] and updated as new research notes are integrated.

## Domain Definitions

### Domain 1 — Scientific Frameworks
**Scope:** Empirical and theoretical models of consciousness from neuroscience, cognitive science, and psychology.  
**Key documents:** [[RP-001 Consciousness Theory Matrix]], individual research notes (pending)  
**Coverage status:** Theory matrix established; research notes pending  
**Primary contributors:** Perplexity, Gemini

---

### Domain 2 — Philosophical Questions
**Scope:** The philosophical dimensions of consciousness — the hard problem, the nature of qualia, the mind-body problem, the self.  
**Key documents:** [[RP-001 Comparative Framework]], [[RP-001 Canonical Glossary]]  
**Coverage status:** Core questions documented; detailed research notes pending  
**Primary contributors:** LUMIAION, Perplexity

---

### Domain 3 — Contemplative Traditions
**Scope:** First-person investigation traditions — their frameworks, their phenomenological findings, and their relationship to scientific research.  
**Key documents:** [[RP-001 Consciousness Theory Matrix]] (contemplative section), [[RP-001 Canonical Glossary]]  
**Coverage status:** Frameworks mapped; phenomenological research notes pending  
**Primary contributors:** LUMIAION, SanaLab, Founder direct reports

---

### Domain 4 — Altered States and Practices
**Scope:** Meditation, psychedelics, flow states, near-death experiences, sleep and dreaming, hypnosis.  
**Coverage status:** Not yet researched; pending first research notes in this domain  
**Primary contributors:** Perplexity, Gemini, SanaLab

---

### Domain 5 — Self and Identity
**Scope:** The neuroscience and phenomenology of selfhood; the constructed self; the minimal self; dissolution of self in contemplative practice.  
**Coverage status:** Foundational concepts in glossary; detailed research pending  
**Primary contributors:** Perplexity, LUMIAION

---

### Domain 6 — Applied Domains
**Scope:** Where consciousness research matters for practice — creativity, education, artificial intelligence, clinical applications (consciousness disorders, anaesthesia, disorders of consciousness).  
**Coverage status:** Not yet researched  
**Note:** Clinical applications overlap with ATHENA's domain; coordination required

---

### Domain 7 — Founder Phenomenological Reports
**Scope:** First-person reports from the Founder (Om Sadhi Guru) documenting his direct experience from meditation, yoga, Reiki, and related practices.  
**Coverage status:** Format not yet established; first reports pending  
**Classification:** All Class P (Phenomenological)  
**Note:** These reports are a unique and institutionally significant data source — direct phenomenological investigation by the person building the institution. They must be documented with the same rigour as other research.
