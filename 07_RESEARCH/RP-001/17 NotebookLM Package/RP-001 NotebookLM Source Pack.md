# RP-001 — NotebookLM Source Pack

---

## Purpose

This document defines and tracks the source materials loaded into Google NotebookLM for RP-001 research synthesis. NotebookLM is used as a deep research tool — loaded with primary academic sources, it provides grounded synthesis that reduces the risk of AI hallucination present when using tools like Perplexity and Gemini without source grounding.

This document specifies what should be in the NotebookLM workspace, what has been loaded, and what the workspace has been used to produce.

---

## Context

AI-generated research (Perplexity, Gemini) is useful but carries the risk noted in [[RP-001 Source Registry]]: claims without traceable primary sources should be treated as Class E or lower. NotebookLM addresses this by working *from* primary sources — books, papers, reports loaded directly into the workspace. Outputs from NotebookLM sessions are more traceable than general AI synthesis.

NotebookLM sessions are treated as a distinct contributor in [[RP-001 Source Registry]] with quality profile: Medium-High (bounded by what is loaded; methodology traceable to the sources loaded).

## Session Protocol

When conducting a NotebookLM research session for RP-001:

1. Confirm the sources relevant to the session topic are loaded
2. Ask the workspace to: (a) summarise the consensus view, (b) identify competing positions, (c) note where sources disagree, (d) flag what they do not cover
3. Record the session date and topic in the Session Log above
4. Produce a draft research note following [[Research Note Template]]
5. Apply evidence classification per [[Book III - Knowledge Integrity]], Art. II
6. Register new sources in [[RP-001 Source Registry]]
7. Submit draft to JERANIUM for integration review before committing to Vault
