## Program Scope (Planned)

RP-005 will map the scientific and philosophical understanding of human intelligence — what it is, how it is structured, how it develops, and what it implies for artificial general intelligence.

**Anticipated coverage:**
- Structure of intelligence (g factor, multiple intelligences, CHCL model)
- Neural correlates of intelligence (parieto-frontal integration theory, network efficiency)
- Fluid vs. crystallized intelligence
- Intelligence and working memory
- Developmental trajectories of intelligence
- Cultural and environmental influences on intelligence expression
- Philosophical debates: what is intelligence? Is it substrate-independent?
- Implications for AGI design
