## Program Scope (Planned)

RP-003 will map the scientific understanding of human learning — how knowledge, skills, and adaptive behaviors are acquired, consolidated, and transferred.

**Anticipated coverage:**
- Hebbian learning and synaptic plasticity
- Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning in biological systems
- Skill acquisition (explicit vs. implicit learning)
- Transfer learning and generalization
- Developmental learning (critical periods, embodied learning)
- Computational models of biological learning
- Implications for AI training architectures
