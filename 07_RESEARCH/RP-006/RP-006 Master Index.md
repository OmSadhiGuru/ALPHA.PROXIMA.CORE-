## Program Scope (Planned)

RP-006 will be the capstone research program of the first generation — mapping the scientific, philosophical, and contemplative understanding of wisdom. It is the most ambitious and least empirically defined of the first six programs.

**Anticipated coverage:**
- Scientific models of wisdom (Berlin Wisdom Paradigm, three-dimensional model)
- Neural correlates of wisdom: prefrontal integration, emotion regulation, perspective-taking
- Wisdom and metacognition; wisdom and emotional intelligence
- Philosophical traditions: Aristotelian phronesis; Stoic sophia; Indian darshana
- Contemplative traditions: wisdom in Advaita Vedanta, Buddhist prajna, Yogic viveka
- The relationship between knowledge, understanding, and wisdom
- Can wisdom be synthesized by an AI? What are the necessary conditions?
- Implications for the design of wise AI systems

## Relationship to Other Programs

RP-006 is the integrative capstone:
- RP-001 (Consciousness) → What does it mean to be aware?
- RP-002 (Memory) → What does it mean to know the past?
- RP-003 (Learning) → What does it mean to grow?
- RP-004 (Decision Making) → What does it mean to choose well?
- RP-005 (Intelligence) → What does it mean to think well?
- **RP-006 (Wisdom) → What does it mean to be good and act well?**
