## Program Scope (Planned)

RP-004 will map the scientific understanding of human decision making — how choices are formed, evaluated, and executed, and how these processes can inform AI decision architectures.

**Anticipated coverage:**
- Dual-process theory (System 1 vs. System 2)
- Prospect theory and behavioral economics
- Neural basis of decision making (orbitofrontal cortex, dopaminergic systems)
- Risk, uncertainty, and ambiguity in decision making
- Moral and ethical decision making
- Active Inference as a decision-making framework
- Implications for AI value alignment and autonomous agents
