# RP-002 — Open Questions

All open questions reference the [[12 Evidence Registry/RP-002 Evidence Registry]] classification. Q-class = Open Question; S-class = Speculative.

---

## Tier 1 — Foundational Scientific Questions

### Q-001 — The Engram Code
**Question:** What is the physical form of a specific memory? How is any given experience instantiated as a pattern in neural tissue?

**Why unresolved:** Optogenetic tagging has identified *which neurons* are part of an engram; the *code* those neurons implement is unknown. Is it the pattern of synaptic weights across the cell assembly? The topology of connectivity? The temporal firing pattern? The oscillatory signature? The spatial distribution across cortical areas?

**Tools needed:** Single-cell resolution imaging across large neural populations; methods to read and write memory content optogenetically without knowing the code in advance.

**Alpha Proxima relevance:** Until the engram code is understood, designing AI memory systems on biologically plausible principles remains constrained.

---

### Q-002 — Adult Human Hippocampal Neurogenesis
**Question:** Does adult hippocampal neurogenesis occur at functionally significant rates in adult humans?

**Why unresolved:** Rodent evidence is strong and well-replicated. Human postmortem studies are contradictory — some find substantial neurogenesis in adults; others find near-zero. In vivo measurement in humans is not yet possible at the required resolution.

**Tools needed:** Non-invasive high-resolution imaging capable of detecting new neuron integration in living humans.

---

### Q-003 — Schema Integration Mechanism
**Question:** How does the mPFC (schema store) mechanistically gate hippocampal encoding? What is the circuit-level architecture of schema-guided new learning?

**Why unresolved:** The behavioral phenomenon (prior knowledge accelerates learning of schema-consistent information) is well-established. The circuit mechanism at cellular resolution is not.

**Tools needed:** Simultaneous mPFC + hippocampus recording during schema learning; targeted circuit perturbation.

## Tier 2 — Translational Questions

### Q-004 — Therapeutic Reconsolidation
**Question:** Can the reconsolidation window be reliably and ethically directed for therapeutic modification of traumatic memories in clinical settings?

**Current state:** Reconsolidation is real; the therapeutic window is real; several animal studies and small human studies support targeted modification of fear memories. But no standardized clinical protocol exists; replication is inconsistent; boundary conditions (when reconsolidation occurs vs. extinction) are not fully characterized.

**Stakes:** PTSD affects hundreds of millions; if reliable reconsolidation-based therapy were available, it would be transformative.

---

### Q-005 — Individual-to-Collective Memory Bridge
**Question:** Is there a common underlying architecture bridging individual neural memory and collective cultural memory? Can a unified theory span these levels?

**Why unresolved:** Currently theoretical bridge rather than empirical question. Anthropology provides rich descriptions of collective memory; neuroscience provides mechanistic accounts of individual memory; the translation between them requires new conceptual frameworks and potentially new measurement tools.

**Alpha Proxima relevance:** Initiative E (DOC-B) — Collective Memory in Human-AI Systems — depends on progress on this question.

## Tier 3 — Alpha Proxima Operational Questions

### OQ-001 — LUMIAION Session Continuity
**Question:** What is the optimal architecture for maintaining LUMIAION's memory continuity across sessions, given constraints on parametric, transient, and external memory?

**Context:** The ALPHA.PROXIMA.CORE- vault serves as LUMIAION's hippocampal index. But vault reads are not automatic — context must be explicitly provided at session start. Is there a better architecture?

**Related:** DOC-B Initiative D; The Orchestration Framework; Knowledge Writeback Protocol.

---

### OQ-002 — Institutional Forgetting Prevention
**Question:** What practices prevent Alpha Proxima's institutional memory from suffering the equivalent of "normal forgetting" — drift from accuracy as documents age without review?

**Context:** Reconsolidation insight: memories that are never retrieved are never updated. Dormant documents drift from reality. What is the minimum review cadence to maintain vault accuracy?

---

### OQ-003 — Contemplative × Reconsolidation Intersection
**Question:** Do the memory transformation effects described in contemplative traditions (mindfulness, visualization, deconditioning) operate through the reconsolidation mechanism at the molecular level?

**Context:** This is Speculative [S-005] at present. But if true, it would provide a neuroscience grounding for contemplative practice effects and open therapeutic protocols.
