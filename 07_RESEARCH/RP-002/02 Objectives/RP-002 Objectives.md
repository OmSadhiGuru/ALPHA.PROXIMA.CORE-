# RP-002 — Objectives

---

## Phase 1 Objectives (Current)

### O-001 — Source Acquisition and Review
Acquire, archive, and fully review all primary sources for the Atlas of Human Memory.
- **Target:** DOC-A ✓, DOC-B ✓, DOC-C ⏳ pending
- **Status:** 2/3 complete

### O-002 — Evidence Classification
Classify all material claims from reviewed sources using the Alpha Proxima evidence taxonomy (C / M / E / Q / S / P).
- **Target:** Complete classification of DOC-A and DOC-B claims
- **Status:** Complete — 31 claims classified in Evidence Registry v1.0.0

### O-003 — Canonical Synthesis
Produce a synthesis document integrating all reviewed sources into a unified, authoritative account of human memory for Alpha Proxima purposes.
- **Target:** 10-chapter canonical synthesis
- **Status:** Complete — Canonical Synthesis v1.0.0; pending DOC-C integration (→ v1.1.0)

### O-004 — Knowledge Graph
Develop a concept note for each of the 10 key terms in the program, forming the RP-002 knowledge graph.
- **Target:** 10 concept notes in `13 Research Graph/Concepts/`
- **Status:** In progress

### O-005 — Theory Matrix
Map all competing theoretical frameworks identified in the source set into a structured Theory Matrix.
- **Target:** Complete Theory Matrix
- **Status:** In progress

### O-006 — Institutional Infrastructure
Complete the full 22-subfolder institutional infrastructure for RP-002, mirroring the RP-001 architecture.
- **Target:** All required documents in all 22 subfolders
- **Status:** In progress
