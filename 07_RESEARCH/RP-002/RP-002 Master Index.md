## Core Finding

> **Memory is reconstructive.** Every act of recall re-creates rather than replays. This is true at the molecular level (reconsolidation), the cognitive level (constructive recall), and the cultural level (collective memory as ongoing social construction). The ALPHA.PROXIMA.CORE- vault is Alpha Proxima's hippocampal index. Its integrity is the integrity of the institution's memory.
