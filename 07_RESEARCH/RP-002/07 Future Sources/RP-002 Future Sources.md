## Identified Source Gaps (from Evidence Registry and Open Questions)

The following knowledge gaps in the current source set suggest directions for future source acquisition:

| Gap | Recommended Source Type | Priority |
|-----|------------------------|---------|
| Non-Western / Indigenous memory traditions (noted as underrepresented in DOC-B) | Anthropological or cross-cultural comparative study | Medium |

| Mathematical / computational memory models (Hopfield networks, Boltzmann machines) | Technical review — computational neuroscience | Low |
| Clinical neuropsychology (amnesia syndromes, HM case full documentation, dementia) | Clinical case study or neuropsychology textbook chapter | Low |
| DOC-C full review — once poppler-utils or equivalent available | Founder review of existing PDF | **High — immediate** |

## Source Evaluation Criteria

Before commissioning a new source for RP-002, it must be assessed against:

1. **Independence:** Does it draw from primary literature independent of DOC-A and DOC-B?
2. **Gap coverage:** Does it address an identified gap in current coverage?
3. **Quality:** Is it peer-reviewed, systematic, or at minimum produced by a reputable research tool?
4. **Relevance:** Does it directly inform the research question or LUMIAION design implications?
5. **Format:** Can it be rendered and fully reviewed in a session? (Large illustrated PDFs require Founder review.)
