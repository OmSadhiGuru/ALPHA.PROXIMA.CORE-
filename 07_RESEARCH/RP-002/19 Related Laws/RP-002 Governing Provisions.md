# RP-002 — Governing Provisions

Provisions that govern how RP-002 is conducted, maintained, and applied within Alpha Proxima.

---

## Provision 1 — ARCHIVE Immutability

Once a source document is registered in the RP-002 ARCHIVE, it may not be modified. Source notes may be updated (versioned); archive registrations carry `immutable: true`. See [[ARCHIVE/ARCHIVE Philosophy]].

## Provision 2 — Evidence Classification Requirement

Every material factual claim extracted from RP-002 sources must be classified using the Alpha Proxima evidence taxonomy (C / M / E / Q / S / P) before being cited in canonical documents. Unclassified claims may not appear in the Canonical Synthesis without classification.

## Provision 3 — DOC-C Integration Gate

The Canonical Synthesis remains at v1.0.0 status (pending) until DOC-C is formally reviewed by the Founder and its content integrated. DOC-C claims must be classified before integration.

## Provision 4 — Research Initiative Development

No Research Initiative (A through E from DOC-B) may be designated "active" without a formal initiative specification document developed through Alpha Proxima's standard research program process.

## Provision 5 — Cross-Program Citation

When RP-002 findings are cited in other programs (RP-001, system design documents, constitutions), the citation must reference the Evidence Registry classification (e.g., [RP-002: C-001]) so readers understand the epistemic status of the claim.
