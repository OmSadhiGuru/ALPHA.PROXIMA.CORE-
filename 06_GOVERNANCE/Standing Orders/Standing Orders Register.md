# Standing Orders Register

---

## Purpose

This is the authoritative register of all Standing Orders (SO-series) issued to institutional offices of the Alpha Proxima Foundation. It records every Standing Order — active, suspended, superseded, and retired — as a permanent institutional memory.

Standing Orders define the continuous operational behavior of an office. They are persistent until replaced. They are not missions. They have no completion date.

See [[Directive Governance Framework]] for the full definition, lifecycle, and governance of Standing Orders.
