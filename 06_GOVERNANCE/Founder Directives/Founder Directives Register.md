# Founder Directives Register

---

## Purpose

This is the authoritative register of all Founder Directives (FD-series) issued at the Alpha Proxima Foundation. It records every Founder Directive — active, completed, suspended, and superseded — as permanent institutional memory.

Founder Directives are bounded missions initiated by the Founder. They are finite, completable, and archived after execution. They are the highest operational authority while active.

See [[Directive Governance Framework]] for the full definition, lifecycle, and governance of Founder Directives.

## Register Notes

- Status transitions are recorded here and in the directive document itself.
