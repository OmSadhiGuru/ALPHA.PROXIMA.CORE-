# Alpha Proxima Core

## Purpose

This is the Map of Content (MOC) and index note for the entire Alpha Proxima Core ecosystem. It is the starting point for navigation, orientation, and onboarding. Every major node in the knowledge graph links back here.

The trailing dash in `ALPHA.PROXIMA.CORE-` is intentional. It signifies that this work is permanently incomplete — there is always a next frontier.

---

## Context

> *Building open architectures for AI orchestration, knowledge systems, digital memory, and human augmentation. Home of [[LUMIAION]].*

Alpha Proxima Core was founded on the belief that the next era of human capability will be built at the intersection of structured knowledge, intelligent systems, and deliberate governance. The project is not a startup, a product, or a research lab in the conventional sense. It is an architecture — for thinking, building, and deciding — that evolves as the work demands.

The project operates under [[Book I - The Constitution]], which is the supreme governing instrument of everything built here.
